Important Legal Information

Confidentiality

All information shared under these agreements must remain confidential and cannot be disclosed to third parties.

Duration

NDAs typically remain in effect for 2-5 years after the agreement period ends.

Enforcement

Violations of NDA terms may result in legal action and financial penalties.
